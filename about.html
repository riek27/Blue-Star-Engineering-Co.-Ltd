About Blue Star Engineering

Leading Samsung appliance provider in South Sudan since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Blue Star Engineering Co. Ltd. has been at the forefront of providing premium Samsung appliances and professional services to the people of South Sudan. What started as a small family business has grown into the nation's most trusted partner for quality air conditioning, power solutions, and home appliances.

Over the past decade, we have built a reputation for reliability, expertise, and exceptional customer service. Our commitment to using only genuine Samsung parts and employing certified technicians has made us the preferred choice for both residential and commercial clients.
